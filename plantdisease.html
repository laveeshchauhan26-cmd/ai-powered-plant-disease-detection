    Knowledge Base

    How It Works

    • User uploads an image of a plant leaf or searches for a disease.
    • The system analyzes the image or query to match possible diseases using its database.
    • It displays the likely disease, symptoms, and suggested treatments.
    • Guidance is provided on disease management and further resources.

    What are Plant Diseases?

    • Plant diseases are disorders caused by pathogens (fungi, bacteria, viruses) or environmental stress.
    • They affect leaves, stems, roots, flowers, or fruit, causing visible symptoms.
    • Disease outbreaks can be triggered by high humidity, poor soil, overcrowding, or pest infestation.

    How Do Plant Diseases Affect Plants?

    • Diseases reduce photosynthesis, stunting plant growth and yield.
    • Can cause leaf yellowing, wilting, spots, or rot, making crops unmarketable.
    • Severe cases may kill plants or spread across farms causing major economic loss.
    • Early detection and proper management are crucial for healthy crops.
